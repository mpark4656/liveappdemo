Opacity & Alpha

Alpha channel controls transparency of the background color. It does not impact the transparency of the contents. The value of alpha channel is from 0 to 1 where 1 is no transparency and 0 is full transparency.

This box has alpha channel of 0.2.

Opacity controls the transparency of all contents, including the border (If there is one) and the child elements.

This box has alpha opacity of 0.2.
